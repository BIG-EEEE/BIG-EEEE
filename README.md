<table>

        🔭 I am a graduate of Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Kumasi, Ghana, with a background in Meteorology and Climate Science. My research focused on the assessment of drivers of rainfall variability in the Northern Region of Ghana. Climate drivers such as ENSO, Land Use and Land Cover Change (LULCC), and the Inter-Tropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ) were analyzed using statistical techniques including the Pearson Correlation Method. Findings from the study revealed that LULCC and ITCZ were the major contributors to rainfall variability in Northern Ghana.
